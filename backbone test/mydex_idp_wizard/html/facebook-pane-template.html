Why do we ask for your Facebook information?

Most people in the UK spend a lot of time online, especially on social networking sites like Facebook or Twitter. This kind of activity one of the best ways to prove who you are.

For example, most Facebook users spend thousands of hours developing their profile over many years, in a way that would be impossible to fake.

Combined with the safety checks and security measures Facebook has developed, this makes signing into your Facebook a better proof of your identity than showing your passport.

Connecting will not:

  • Post anything to your timeline
  • Share your Facebook information with anyone else
  • Store your personal information with anyone else
