Accessing government services online with Mydex

You're registering to use your Mydex account to access government services online.

To do this, you'll retrieve data that other organisations hold about you, to confirm your identity from multiple sources.

You can then use this combined information to create an identity profile that the government will accept as proof of your identity.

When you sign using Mydex to access government services, this identity profile will act as proof of your identity.
